Pricing, Licensing & Engagement

Transparent answers about implementation costs, engagement models, Salesforce license selection, AppExchange apps, and how we measure the value we deliver.

10 questions answered
01

Do you work with nonprofits?

Yes. A significant portion of our portfolio consists of nonprofits. We are uniquely tailored to handle tight budgets, limited staffing, and high operational complexity.

02

How much will a typical Salesforce engagement cost?

Small projects typically run $8k–$16k. Medium and large builds run $21k–$63k+. Retainers scale from $2k–$4k/mo (small) to $8k–$12k/mo (enterprise).

03

Do you offer fixed-price projects, hourly blocks, or monthly retainers?

We offer all three: fixed-price for clearly defined, scope-frozen projects; hourly blocks for short-term burst work; and monthly retainers for proactive support.

04

What is included in an initial project estimate versus what becomes a change order?

Estimates cover discovery, configuration, testing, and training. Change orders typically stem from mid-project integration additions, messy data rescue, or major layout pivots.

05

Which Salesforce edition and add-ons do we need?

It varies based on size. Small nonprofits often start on NPSP, while scaling entities utilize Nonprofit Cloud. Portals require Experience Cloud. We run license reviews to map this.
