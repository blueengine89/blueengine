Portals, Programs & Field Operations

Answers about building Experience Cloud portals, tracking program enrollments and outcomes, managing volunteers and events, routing field teams, and fixing email deliverability.

13 questions answered

Can you build external-facing portals or Experience Cloud sites?

Yes. We build customized digital portals allowing clients, grantees, or members to securely log in, interact with records, and review document statuses.

Can you design a custom grantee, client, or volunteer portal experience?

Yes. We build tailored routing flows that manage application inputs, volunteer hours logging, program registrations, and programmatic outcome reporting.

What is the right architecture for our portal (Experience Cloud vs. third-party form system)?

If you need deep customization and native Single Sign-On (SSO), Experience Cloud is best. For fast, low-cost collection, an integrated form approach works well.

How do you manage licensing for portal users versus per-login billing costs?

We model your user behavior: high-frequency users get assigned a dedicated monthly seat license, while occasional visitors are routed to cost-effective per-login pools.

Can you track program enrollments, service deliveries, and operational outcomes?

Yes. We build custom data tracking pipelines or leverage standard patterns like the Program Management Module (PMM) to trace participant service lifecycles.

How do you design digital assessments, scoring frameworks, and printable staff reports?

We build guided Screen Flows for data collection, auto-calculate programmatic scores, and integrate document engines (Conga/S-Docs) to generate clean PDFs.

What is your approach for handling multi-member households or multi-program participation?

We architect junction object relationships and automated looping flows to evaluate complex relational networks like multi-children households or cross-program enrollments.

How can we automate event attendee imports, volunteer hours, and field-team photo capture?

We sync automated batch processors for event check-ins, build rapid mobile logging forms for volunteer hours, and deploy specialized mobile photo storage tools.

What solutions exist for asset routing and field scheduling (Salesforce Maps) and syncing to calendars?

We implement Salesforce Maps for geometric routing optimization and configure Einstein Activity Capture to sync field logs automatically with Outlook or Gmail.

How do you minimize manual administrative steps for high-volume events or mass data imports?

We establish standardized CSV upload templates, map automated data loader scripts, and leverage programmatic deduplication steps to process mass records.

Why aren't our system emails reaching recipients, and how do you diagnose deliverability?

Issues typically stem from unconfigured server protocols. We analyze system mail headers and configure proper domain authentications (SPF, DKIM, DMARC).

Do you recommend a dedicated IP address or system upgrades in Pardot/Marketing tools?

Dedicated IPs are valuable for mass mail volumes (100k+/mo). We audit your actual deliverability patterns before recommending a costly IP warming track.

How do you sync marketing lists and handle complex communication subscription preferences?

We set Salesforce as your operational source of truth, map preference fields to marketing engines, and construct custom email subscription preference centers.
