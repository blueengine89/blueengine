Common Questions

Before you fill out the form.

Is this actually free? What's the catch?
Genuinely free. No invoice at the end, no credit card required, no pressure to hire us afterward. We offer it because it's a great way to meet potential clients, and honestly — we just find it interesting. You'll get real value regardless of what happens next.
Do you need admin access to our Salesforce org?
For a thorough review, read-only system admin access is ideal. But we can work with whatever you're comfortable sharing — even a screen-share walkthrough gives us a lot to work with. We'll discuss access when we schedule the debrief.
How long does the review take?
We typically complete the review within 3–5 business days of receiving access, then schedule a 30-minute debrief call to walk through findings. Total elapsed time from form submission to debrief is usually under one week.
What if we're a small nonprofit with a basic setup?
Perfect — about half our clients are nonprofits, many with small teams and tight budgets. Simpler orgs often have cleaner findings that are faster to act on. We'll scope the review appropriately and be upfront if there isn't much to flag.
What happens after the debrief?
Entirely up to you. Take the report, share it with your team, and handle things yourself. Or if the findings point to work you'd like help with, we can talk about next steps. There's no obligation and we won't follow up with sales emails.
